About Me

Web3 Developer, Researcher, and Educator

I am a seasoned Web3 developer with a rich background in research, boasting several published papers on Web3 advancements. Since entering the Web3 domain in 2019, I have been a dynamic contributor to various Web3 communities, with a particular focus on the Ethereum blockchain.

My artistic prowess is recognized on platforms like Rarible and Litemint, where I am a verified NFT artist. With the inception of Parabolic DAO, I am at the forefront of Web3 education, offering a pathway for individuals to align their careers with the evolving digital landscape.

Parabolic DAO leverages its active collaborations with leading Web3 projects to provide unique job opportunities in the field. I am also a lecturer in Modibbo Adama University, Yola Nigeria, department of Civil Engineering.
